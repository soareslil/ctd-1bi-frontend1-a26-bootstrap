About me

Liliane is a self-taught student who is learning web and mobile development and always looking forward to join and create innovative projects around people from different cultures, backgrounds and mindsets. She is a latina from Brazil who loves to overcome social challenges and lives to inspire black women around the world. She has a resilient mind that gives her the power to move forward while thinking about her long-term achievements. When she was in school she received a scholarship sponsored by the US government to join the Cultural Association Brazil-United States right after she started her minor in humanities. There, she studied about culture, society, psychology and technology.

During her second year in her University (UFSB) she started to look for opportunities so she could go abroad and she ended up being the sole Brazilian accepted in 2019-2020 to be part of the Sutardja Center for Entrepreneurship and Technology inside the prestigious UC Berkeley where she was able to explore, live, and intern around and inside of Silicon Valley for tech startups that were part of Berkeley Skydeck, Schoolab and others. Today, Liliane is interning for a real estate company in Brazil.
